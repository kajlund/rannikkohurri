I’m Kaj Lund a.k.a LuKa, a partly self-taught programmer that has been employed developing business software since 1998.

Over the years I’ve fiddled around with languages like Basic, Turbo Pascal, C++, Java, Delphi, C#, Python, Ruby and, as of lately, JavaScript.

Back in 1999 we adopted a then pretty unique application framework/ORM called Bold for Delphi, and in doing so we adopted what is now known as Domain-Driven Design well before that term was invented by Eric Evans in 2004.

If there is some general observation I’ve done over the years it is that we never seem to stop believing that there’s a silver bullet to be found for software development by using the latest language or framework. I don’t believe that there is such a silver bullet to be found, but I find it very rewarding to keep looking just to learn new technology.

I consider myself being more of a Technology/Developer evangelist than a hard core programmer.
